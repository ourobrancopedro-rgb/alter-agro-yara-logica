The marks **“YARA Lógica”** and **“Alter Agro”** are trademarks of Alter Agro Ltda. Use of these marks is subject to the rules below.

## Permitted Nominative Use

You may refer to the marks in descriptive text when:
- Accurately describing Alter Agro’s products or specifications.
- Referencing the YARA Lógica public specification repository.
- Citing Alter Agro as the source of documentation or policies.

All nominative use must include proper capitalization and the accent in “Lógica.”

## Prohibited Uses

- Using the marks in product names, project names, or package identifiers without written permission.
- Registering domains or social media handles incorporating the marks.
- Using logos, stylized marks, or other Alter Agro brand assets.
- Implying partnership, endorsement, or certification without a signed agreement.

## Attribution

When referencing the marks, include the statement: “YARA Lógica and Alter Agro are trademarks of Alter Agro Ltda.”
